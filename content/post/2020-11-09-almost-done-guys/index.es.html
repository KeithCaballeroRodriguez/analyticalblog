Aplicamos exponencial

\[f(x)=\exp \{-\beta x+\alpha \log \beta+(\alpha-1) \log x-\log \Gamma(\alpha)\}\]

\[=\exp \left\{\frac{-\frac{\beta}{\alpha} x+\frac{\alpha}{\alpha} \log \beta}{\frac{1}{\alpha}}+(\alpha-1) \log x-\log \Gamma(\alpha)\right\}\]

\[\beta=\theta_{\alpha}=\frac{\theta}{\phi} \] \[\log \beta=\log \theta-\log \phi \] \[=\exp \left\{\frac{\theta x-\log \theta}{-\phi}+\frac{\log \phi}{\phi}+\left(\frac{1}{\Phi}-1\right) \log x-\log \left(\Gamma\left(\frac{1}{\phi}\right)\right)\right] \] \[=\exp \left\{\frac{\theta x-\log \theta}{-\phi}+c(x, \phi)\right\} \] \[b(\theta)=\log \theta \quad a(\phi)=-\phi\] ## Si queremos obtener tanto la esperanza como la varianza de las expresiones en funci贸n a lo obtenido entonces:
